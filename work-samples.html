My Work

Below are selected samples of my academic work spanning research, digital media production, and web development. Each project was completed independently unless otherwise noted.

Composition II

Food Insecurity at Northwest Missouri State University

This project was the culmination of a semester-long research effort examining food insecurity among college students, with a focus on Northwest Missouri State University and the surrounding Maryville community. The website serves as both an informational resource and a call to action, presenting research findings, local statistics, and available resources in an accessible and shareable format. The project involved independent research, academic writing, web design, and digital publishing.

Digital Media

Songs of World War II

This video slideshow project was created for a Digital Media course and significantly exceeded the baseline requirements of the assignment. The project presents a curated collection of songs from the World War II era, pairing musical selections with historical imagery and context to create an immersive and informative viewing experience. Spanning over 37 minutes, the project required extensive research, media curation, and video production skills to execute at a professional level.

Digital Media

Podcast: AI in the Workplace

This podcast episode was produced for a Digital Media course with the goal of creating a professional-quality audio presentation on a topic relevant to the student's major. The episode explores the growing role of artificial intelligence in the modern workplace, examining its implications for business operations, employment, and professional development. The project involved scripting, recording, audio editing, and production to achieve a polished final product.

Digital Media

Fountain Video Montage

This video montage was created for a Digital Media course assignment in which students were provided a selection of pre-recorded short video clips and tasked with assembling them into a cohesive, professional-quality final product. The project focused on developing video editing skills including clip selection, sequencing, pacing, and visual storytelling. The finished montage showcases the Bellagio Fountains in Las Vegas, edited to create a visually engaging and rhythmically structured viewing experience.
