Who I Am

I am a Business-Technology student at Northwest Missouri State University, originally from Maryville, Missouri. My academic journey has not been a straight line. After graduating from Maryville High School in 2018, I initially pursued a degree in History before stepping away from college in 2022. Returning in 2024 with a clearer sense of direction, I began studying Computer Science before pivoting into Business-Technology.

My Journey

That decision was intentional. I wanted a degree that combined analytical and technical skills with real-world business application, and one that offered broad career flexibility rather than locking me into a single narrow field.

Looking Ahead

My goal after graduation is to build a foundation of professional experience in the business-technology space, ideally starting locally in the Maryville area. I see my first few years out of school as an opportunity to develop practical expertise across a range of business and technology functions. From there I plan to pursue more advanced roles as my experience grows, with an openness to where those opportunities may lead.

Beyond the Resume

Outside of academics and work I have a long-standing interest in strategy and tactical video games. Skills like planning ahead, managing resources, and adapting to changing conditions translate naturally into how I approach problems academically and professionally.
